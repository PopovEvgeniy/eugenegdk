The controls for a keyboard

The arrow keys: Move.
The space key: Exit from the demo.
The left mouse button: Exit from the demo.
The F1 key: Disable the background mirroring.
The F2 key: Do the horizontal background mirror.
The F3 key: Do the vertical background mirror.
The F4 key: Do both the horizontal and the vertical background mirrors.
The F5 key: Enable FPS limit.
The F6 key: Disable FPS limit.

The controls for a gamepad

The D-pad or the right stick: Move.
The button 2: Exit from the demo.
